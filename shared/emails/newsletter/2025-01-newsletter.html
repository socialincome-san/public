AI Time Trap

It still feels too early to tell how AI is affecting the quality of my life. Sure, I get the theory that machines are supposed to free up time for more interesting tasks. (Didn’t they say the same about washing machines and Excel?) But in practice, it seems to have the opposite effect. I use AI tools, thinking I am being more productive, but in the end, I am just cramming more into the same amount of time.

Is this really progress?

Lessons learned

With my university days ending, here’s a question from a recent case study that stuck with me: How do you reintegrate child soldiers into society? Not an easy task. After its devastating civil war in Sierra Leone, over 7,000 children were disarmed and reintegrated into society through United Nations programs.

Even UN critics have to admit: Well done.
